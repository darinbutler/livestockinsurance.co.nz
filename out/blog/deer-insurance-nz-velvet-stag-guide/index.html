Deer Insurance NZ: Protecting Your Velvet and Venison Operation

Specialist guide to deer insurance in New Zealand. Insuring velvet stags, breeding hinds, and your deer herd against mortality, disease, and theft.

Deer Insurance in New Zealand

NZ deer farming is a specialist industry producing velvet for the Asian market and venison for global restaurants. With approximately 800,000 deer farmed in NZ, the industry is worth over $500M annually. Elite velvet stags can be worth $5,000–$50,000+ each — but many deer farmers carry inadequate insurance.

The Unique Risks of Deer Farming

Deer present unique insurance challenges:

  • They are highly valuable individually (especially stags)
  • They are difficult to handle, increasing injury risk
  • Velvet removal is a medical procedure with mortality risk
  • They are agile and frequently escape, creating liability risk
  • Their value is tied to genetics, which is difficult to replace

    • Velvet Stag Insurance

    The most important insurance priority for NZ deer farmers is adequate cover for velvet stags. FMG specifically offers cover for stag death as a result of antler or velvet removal procedures — a unique NZ-specific risk.

    For velvet stag cover:

  • Use agreed value cover, not market value
  • Include velvet removal procedure mortality
  • Consider transit cover for stags used in breeding programmes
  • Review values annually as stag genetics appreciates

    • Breeding Hind Insurance

    While less individually valuable than stags, breeding hinds represent significant herd value in aggregate. Blanket mortality cover is appropriate for commercial hind herds, with disease & illness cover as an add-on.

    Deer Escape Liability

    Deer can clear most standard fences and are fast movers. A deer on a road poses a serious accident risk. Farm & livestock liability cover is important for all deer farmers, especially those near main roads.

    Which Provider for Deer Insurance?

    FMG is the most experienced with NZ deer farming, offering specific velvet stag cover and understanding the unique risks.

    Gallagher is worth consulting for high-value elite genetics stags that may exceed standard FMG agreed value limits.

    Aon via Lloyd's can access Lloyd's markets for specialist agreed value cover on very high-value deer.
